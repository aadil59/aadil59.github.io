Marketing Consulting

We work with you to create and implement marketing strategies. These strategies are centred on the core of the business and products and services that you offer. We help you to create a detailed marketing plan, determine the marketing message, and identify the appropriate marketing mix to get the message out. We will then follow the plan through, and work to execute and implement the marketing strategy. We will monitor results, tweak as necessary and ensure that you get the best results for your marketing investments.

Demand Generation

StrategINK provides a comprehensive suite of demand generation services that span the entire sales lifecycle - from inquiry generation, to lead potential assessment, lead nurturing and post sales engagement, to help you build customer intimacy and expand the scope and scale of your business engagements. We not only generate measurable, actionable interest in your brand, product or service, we also help move prospects along the sales cycle, and then reinforce customer relationships over time through customised content, marketing collaterals and virtual events.

Digital Marketing

We help customers devise a digital marketing strategy and run campaigns in line with their most important business objectives - to help generate inquiries, build sales leads, engage with consumers and key influencers, generate databases and trigger online actions (downloads, event registrations, sales). We employ effective digital marketing tools comprising engaging content (blogs, case studies, customer testimonials etc.) and popular social media channels that boast reach in millions across demographics (youth, women, luxury goods shoppers, airline travellers, cruise holiday and adventure sports enthusiasts et al) and geographical barriers (India, South Asia, South East Asia and Middle East).

Marketing Collateral

The StrategINK expert team of designers, visualisers, copywriters and video editors have consistently produced high quality, impactful collateral for client campaigns - brochures, PoS displays, standees, exhibition booths, corporate videos and much more. We would be happy to engage on your specific requirements and help you produce and distribute the most suitable material to convey the key marketing messages for your brand, product or service.

Virtual Environment
